About Everything Deduper

Everything Deduper is a fast, lightweight tool for finding and removing duplicate files on Windows. It scans your folders using efficient hashing and smart comparison rules, then lets you safely preview files before deleting them.

  • Finds exact duplicate files quickly
  • Safe preview window before deleting anything
  • Portable — no installation required
  • Works on Windows 10 and later
